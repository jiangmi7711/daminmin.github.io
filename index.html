About me

I am a creative and highly motivated Product engineer experienced in designing customer-specific web applications and cooperating with cross-functional teams to ensure successful product and feature delivery.

Throughout my career, I have consistently demonstrated success in driving product adoption, increasing customer satisfaction, and leading cross-functional teams. I am passionate about leveraging technology to solve real-world environmental and safety challenges.

Prior to that, I worked in the permitting and compliance to promote regulatory performance of air quality. I demonstrated my excellent communication skills and writing skills by writing permit conditions, presenting permit requirements and compliance strategies to a variety of people.

In addition to my professional experience, my educational background gives me a diverse set of engineering skills that I will bring to the workplace. I hold dual Master’s degrees, in both Chemistry and Environmental Science, and a bachelor of Chemical Engineering. I also posed professional engineer certificate from California. I believe my deep cross-disciplined background will allow me to assess, communicate, align and implement product solutions meeting different customers’ needs.
